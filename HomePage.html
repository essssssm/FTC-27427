About Our Team

Team Vienna Voltage (FTC 27427) is a community-organized robotics team created to promote STEM to the community and to empower more people through robotics. We are an ambitious group of high school students who not only seek the excitement of participating in competitions, but also strive to spread our knowledge and passion for robotics throughout the community.

Our Mission

Inspire


Encourage younger students to discover STEM fields through engaging robotics activities and demonstrations.

Teach


Provide beginner-friendly robotics education and mentorship for elementary and middle school students.

Innovate


Develop engineering, programming, and teamwork skills while competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge.

How You Can Help

We are seeking partnerships with schools, libraries, community organizations, and local businesses. Support from the community allows us to expand outreach programs, purchase equipment, and create more opportunities for students to explore robotics.
