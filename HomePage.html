About Us

Team Vienna Voltage (FTC 27427) is a community-organized robotics team created to promote STEM to the community and to empower more people through robotics. We are an ambitious group of high schoolers who not only seek the fun in participating in competitions, but also strive to spread our knowledge and passion for robotics to others.

Our Mission

To achieve our goal, we plan to partner with elementary and middle schools to provide thorough, beginner-friendly opportunities for students to explore robotics. Through outreach, mentorship, and hands-on learning experiences, we hope to inspire the next generation of innovators, engineers, and problem-solvers.

How You Can Help

We welcome support from schools, community organizations, local businesses, and individuals who share our passion for STEM education. Your support helps us expand outreach programs, purchase equipment, and create meaningful learning opportunities for students.
