Who We Are

Team Vienna Voltage (FTC 27427) is a community-organized robotics team created to promote STEM within our community and empower more students through robotics. We are an ambitious group of high school students who not only enjoy the excitement of competition but are passionate about sharing our knowledge and inspiring others to explore engineering, programming, and innovation.

Our Mission

Educate

Introduce students to robotics through beginner-friendly lessons, demonstrations, and hands-on experiences.

Inspire

Show young learners how STEM can be exciting, creative, and impactful.

Empower

Provide opportunities for students to gain confidence through teamwork, problem-solving, and engineering.
